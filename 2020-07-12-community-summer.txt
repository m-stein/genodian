                        Genode Community Summer 2020

_Update: Due to the uncertain situation with the Corona virus and the low_
_number of participation requests so far, the Genode Community Summmer 2020_
_will not take place!_

The Genode Community Summer will take place again this year in the lower floor
of the Genode Labs office from 31.8.2020 till 11.9.2020. The gathering is meant
for people who like to work on their Genode project while beeing able to seek
the assistance of other Genode enthusiasts and the guys of Genode Labs in
person.

For further information please see the dedicated
[https://github.com/genodelabs/genode/issues/3818 - Github issue]
that we've just created.
